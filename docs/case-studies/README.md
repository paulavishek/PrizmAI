# PrizmAI Case Studies

These are end-to-end, narrative walkthroughs of running a real project in PrizmAI — from
kickoff to closeout — told through a named project manager in a specific industry. They're
written for **new users**, in plain language, and their goal is to show you *when* and *why*
to reach for PrizmAI's advanced features (Pre-Mortem, Scope Autopsy, Stress Test, What-If &
Shadow Boards, the Decision Center, and Exit Protocol), not just that those features exist.

## Start here if you're new

Read the case study closest to your world — you'll recognize the workflow, and every step
names the actual screen or button so you can follow along in your own board. All three cover
the same core PrizmAI toolkit; they differ in how the story is framed and how it ends.

| If you run… | Read | Leans hardest on | How it ends |
|---|---|---|---|
| Marketing / creative campaigns | **[Marketing Campaign](marketing-campaign.md)** | Budget & ROI, then the full **Exit Protocol** | Campaign is cut mid-flight, then salvaged and relaunched |
| Software / product delivery | **[Software Product Delivery](software-product-delivery.md)** | **Requirements traceability** + Explainable AI | Ships on time with a provable "we delivered what we promised" trail |
| Construction / field projects | **[Construction Project](construction-project.md)** | **Gantt & dependencies** + Stress Test | Hands over on schedule and on budget |

Not sure which fits? The **Marketing** case study is the most complete tour — it's the only
one that walks through Exit Protocol's full wind-down — so it's a good place to start even if
you're not in marketing.

## What these are not

These are illustrative walkthroughs with realistic, replicable steps — not seeded demo data
you'll find pre-loaded in the app, and not domain-specific templates. The personas (Dana,
Sofia, Ray) are fictional. Every feature, screen, and behavior described is real and
verifiable in the product.

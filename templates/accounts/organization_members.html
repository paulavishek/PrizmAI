Organization Role Permissions

Understanding the different roles and their capabilities within your organization.

Owner

Who: The person who created the organization ({{ organization.created_by.username }})

Capabilities:

  • All Admin permissions
  • Can promote and demote any member (including other Admins)
  • Can remove any member from the organization
  • Can delete the entire organization
  • Cannot be removed or demoted by anyone
Admin

Who: Promoted by the Owner or other Admins

Capabilities:

  • Access organization settings
  • View and manage all organization members
  • Promote regular Members to Admin
  • Invite new members to the organization
  • Remove regular Members (not other Admins)
  • Manage roles and permissions
  • Create, edit, and delete boards
  • Full access to all board features and tasks
  • View permission audit logs
Note: Only the Owner can demote an Admin or remove them from the organization.
Member

Who: Default role for new organization members

Capabilities:

  • View boards they are added to
  • Create and manage tasks on their boards
  • Comment on tasks and collaborate
  • Upload and manage files
  • Edit their own profile
  • Use AI features and analytics
Note: Members cannot access organization settings, manage other members, or delete boards.

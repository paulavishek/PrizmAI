What is the What-If Scenario Analyzer?

A safe sandbox where you can simulate the cascading impact of scope changes, team size adjustments, and deadline shifts — before committing to them. PrizmAI computes your live project baseline, then models what would happen under your hypothetical. You get a feasibility score, detected conflicts, and an AI strategic recommendation — all without touching your real board.

The three levers you can pull
Scope ±20 tasks

Add or remove up to 20 tasks from the baseline. Affects velocity, timeline, and workload distribution.

Team Size ±5 members

Add or remove up to 5 team members. Warning: Brooks’s Law — adding people late can slow things down before they help.

Deadline ±8 weeks

Shift the deadline forward or back by up to 8 weeks. Affects schedule pressure, budget burn rate, and feasibility.

What you get after analyzing
  • Feasibility Score (0–100) — how likely the simulated scenario is to succeed
  • Before / After comparison — baseline vs scenario side-by-side
  • Detected Conflicts — any resource overloads, deadline issues, or budget overruns
  • AI Recommendation — Gemini’s strategic take on the trade-offs
Feasibility bands
FEASIBLE 80–100 • Plan is solid, proceed with confidence
VIABLE 60–79 • Achievable with careful management
RISKY 40–59 • Significant constraints, address conflicts
INFEASIBLE 0–39 • Major rework required before proceeding
Detected Conflicts

Conflicts are structural problems the AI found in your simulated scenario. They must be resolved for the plan to be viable.

Resource Overload — team members assigned more hours than they can work in the timeline.

Deadline Infeasibility — remaining work cannot be completed in the available time at current velocity.

Budget Overrun — projected spend exceeds the allocated budget under this scenario.

Recommended workflow
  1. Adjust the three sliders to model your scenario
  2. Click Analyze Impact — get feasibility score + conflicts
  3. Review the AI strategic recommendation
  4. Save interesting scenarios for comparison
  5. Promote to Shadow Branch to make it a living parallel simulation

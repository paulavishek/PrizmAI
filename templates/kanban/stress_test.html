What is the Stress Test?

The Red Team AI reads your live project data and deliberately tries to break your plan. It invents the five most damaging failure scenarios specific to your project, scores your resilience from 0–100, and prescribes structural fixes. Think of it as a fire drill — better to discover the weakness now than when it happens for real.

Attack Scenarios Left column

Hypothetical futures the AI simulated against your plan. None of these have happened yet — the AI invented them by analysing your weakest points.

Mark Addressed means: "I’ve read this, I understand the risk, and I’m actively watching for it." It’s an acknowledgment — you’re not saying it’s fixed, just that it’s on your radar.

Score impact: +3 to +8 pts per acknowledged scenario.

Vaccines Right column

Structural changes the AI prescribes to close the specific gap that made each attack possible. These are real, concrete project changes — not just awareness.

Mark as Applied means: "We have actually made this structural change." The AI banks this permanently and stops attacking that weakness — finding new ones instead.

Score impact: +8 to +20 pts per vaccine applied. Much higher than Addressing alone.

Recommended workflow
  1. Run the Stress Test & read all five attacks carefully
  2. Apply vaccines where you can make the real structural change
  3. Mark scenarios addressed for risks you’re monitoring
  4. Re-run the test — the AI will score your progress and find new weak spots
  5. Repeat until you reach your target band

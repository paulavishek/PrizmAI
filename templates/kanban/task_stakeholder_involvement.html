Understanding Stakeholder Involvement

What are stakeholders? Anyone interested in or affected by this board/project.

Involvement Types (for specific tasks):

  • Task Owner: Person responsible for completing this task
  • Contributor: Actively working on the task
  • Reviewer: Reviewing/quality checking task output
  • Approver/Decision Maker: Must approve or make decisions about the task
  • Observer/Monitor: Watching progress, needs to stay informed
  • Beneficiary: Will directly benefit from task completion
  • Impacted Party: Affected by task outcome or changes

Tip: First create stakeholders for your board (people relevant to the project), then assign them to specific tasks with their role in that task.

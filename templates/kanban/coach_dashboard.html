What is the AI Coach?

A proactive AI mentor that reads your live board data daily and surfaces the highest-priority coaching interventions before small issues become crises. It doesn’t wait for you to ask — it automatically detects blockers, overloaded team members, missed dependencies, overdue tasks, and scope drift, then gives you concrete next steps to fix each one.

Issues Detected

The AI scans your board and classifies every problem it finds into one of four categories:

OVERDUE Tasks past their deadline, blocking progress
BLOCKER Dependency or resource preventing forward movement
RISK Likely problem in the next 1–2 weeks if unaddressed
DRIFT Scope or direction diverging from the original plan
Coaching Actions

For each issue, the AI prescribes a concrete action — not vague advice, but a specific next step:

  • Reassign — move a task to a less loaded team member
  • Negotiate deadline — propose a revised due date with rationale
  • Split task — break an overloaded item into smaller deliverables
  • Escalate — flag to stakeholders when a blocker needs external resolution
Project health bands
CRITICAL Immediate action required today
AT RISK Address this week before it escalates
WATCH Monitor — could develop into a problem
HEALTHY No action needed, project is on track

Recommended workflow
  1. Click Refresh Suggestions to get a fresh set of coaching insights
  2. Start with Critical cards — these need immediate action
  3. Drill into each card to see the affected tasks and the recommended action
  4. Take the action on your board, then come back and click Acknowledge on the suggestion card
  5. Use Provide Feedback to rate suggestions as helpful or not helpful — this trains the AI over time

Tip: The coaching effectiveness score improves as you interact with suggestions. The more you use it, the more targeted it becomes for your project style.

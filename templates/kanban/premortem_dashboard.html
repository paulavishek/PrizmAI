What is Pre-Mortem Analysis?

Before a single line of code is written or a task is started, the AI imagines your project has already failed — then works backwards to explain exactly how it happened. You get five distinct failure scenarios specific to your project, each with a risk level, root-cause explanation, and a concrete mitigation strategy. Think of it as buying insurance before you need it.

Failure Scenarios AI-generated

Five plausible ways your specific project could collapse — invented by the AI by analysing your tasks, deadlines, dependencies, and team allocation. None of these have happened yet.

HIGH Severe, likely to derail the project
MEDIUM Significant, needs active monitoring
LOW Minor, manageable with awareness
Team Acknowledgment Sign-off tracking

Each team member signs off on scenarios they've read and understood. The dashboard tracks who has acknowledged which risks — so no critical warning goes unseen.

Unacknowledged — this risk is invisible to your team. Nobody is watching for it.

Acknowledged — team is actively watching for this risk. Early warning system is armed.

Recommended workflow
  1. Run the Pre-Mortem analysis — AI reads your live board data
  2. Read all 5 failure scenarios carefully, including the mitigation strategy
  3. Team members acknowledge each scenario they've understood
  4. Implement the mitigation strategies for HIGH-risk scenarios
  5. Feed results into the Stress Test for deeper resilience analysis

Tip: Run Pre-Mortem before scope locks in. A 15-minute review now can prevent weeks of damage later.

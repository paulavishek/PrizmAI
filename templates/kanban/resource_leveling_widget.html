AI Scoring Methodology

Each team member is scored on 5 factors for every task, and the highest-scoring member is recommended. When someone is overloaded (>90% capacity), relieving that bottleneck takes priority — so a less-loaded teammate may be suggested even if another would score marginally higher.

FactorWeightWhat it measures
Skill Match30%Overlap between member's skills and task keywords
Availability25%Remaining capacity (100% minus current utilization)
Velocity20%Historical task completion speed
Reliability15%On-time completion rate from past tasks
Quality10%Quality rating of completed work

Members without task history receive a confidence discount. Add skills to team profiles and complete tasks to improve AI accuracy.
